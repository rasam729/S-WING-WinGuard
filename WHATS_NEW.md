# 🎉 What's New in WinGuard - UI/UX Enhancement

## ✨ Major Visual Improvements

### 1. **Professional Typography**
Your app now uses **Clarendon font** for all headings, giving it a professional, authoritative look. Body text uses Inter for excellent readability.

### 2. **Enhanced Color Contrast**
All text and UI elements now meet **WCAG AA accessibility standards** with proper contrast ratios. The cyan-green and orange color scheme is maintained but with improved visibility.

### 3. **Professional Buttons**
- Gradient backgrounds (cyan-to-teal, orange-to-amber)
- Smooth hover effects with scale and shadow
- Clear active/disabled states
- Professional appearance

### 4. **Beautiful Animations**
- Smooth 60fps animations throughout
- Fade-in effects for new content
- Hover lift effects on cards
- Stagger animations for lists
- Professional loading spinners

### 5. **Enhanced Cards & Components**
- Deeper shadows with hover effects
- Gradient card variants
- Professional icon containers
- High-contrast badges
- Glass morphism effects

## 🚀 How to See the Changes

### Option 1: Refresh Your Browser
1. Open your browser
2. Go to http://localhost:5176 (Dashboard) or http://localhost:5173 (Citizen App)
3. Press `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac) to hard refresh
4. Enjoy the new professional design!

### Option 2: Clear Cache
1. Open browser DevTools (F12)
2. Right-click the refresh button
3. Select "Empty Cache and Hard Reload"
